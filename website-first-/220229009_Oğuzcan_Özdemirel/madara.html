You choose Madara

Madara is a prominent member of the Uchiha clan who helped to found Konoha alongside the First Hokage. However, as the leader of his clan, he felt that he and his people were not being treated equally with other clans in Konoha and decided to leave. Madara later became a powerful ninja and acted alone to save the Uchiha clan in the midst of the war. Eventually, he accepted defeat and made peace with his friend, Hashirama Senju. In later years, Madara resurfaced during the Second Ninja War and fought against Konoha once again. However, he was ultimately defeated by Naruto and his allies and died.
